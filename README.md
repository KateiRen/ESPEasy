AM2302 / DHT22

AM2302 Pin | AM2302 Signal 
-------| ------- 
 1 | VCC
 2 | Data
 3 | not connected
 4 | GND

ESP01-S1 Pin | ESP01 Signal 
-------| ------- 
 1 | TXD
 2 | CH_PD
 3 | RST
 4 | VCC
 5 | GND
 6 | GPIO2
 7 | GPIO0
 8 | RST

OLED Pin | OLED Signal 
-------| ------- 
 1 | VCC
 2 | GND
 3 | SCL
 4 | SDA
