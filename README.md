# ESPEasy

AM2302 / DHT22

AM2302 Pin | AM2302 Signal 
-------| ------- 
 1 | VCC
 2 | Data
 3 | not connected
 4 | GND

ESP01-S1 Pin | ESP01 Signal 
-------| ------- 
 1 | TXD / GPIO1
 2 | CH_PD
 3 | RST
 4 | VCC
 5 | GND
 6 | GPIO2
 7 | GPIO0
 8 | RXD / GPIO3

OLED Pin | OLED Signal 
-------| ------- 
 1 | VCC
 2 | GND
 3 | SCL
 4 | SDA


LED an GPIO1
OLED SCL an GPIO2
OLED SDA an GPIO0
DHT22 an GPIO3
